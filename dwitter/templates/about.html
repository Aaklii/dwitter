About dwitter.net


Dwitter is a platform to create and share visual art written in 140 characters of javascript. It was created by u/lionleaf as a side project inspired by the concept of u/sigveseb at arkt.is/t (github). Its first official launch was at the Demoparty 'Solskogen' in 2016 where it won the wild compo.


The website itself is open source, and is being developed by a number of contributors. We are happy to accept pull requests and issues on github.
Check out the issue tracker for previous suggestions and discussions.


Please join us in the discord chat to hang out, ask for coding help, or discuss anything :D See you there!
